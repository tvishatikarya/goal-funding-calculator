Goal-Based Funding Calculator

Enter your goals below, then run the simulation to see your risk-adjusted chances of hitting each one. Built for people who plan their own money instead of relying on an advisor.

This is your real-world budget ceiling — what you can actually set aside every month, split across every goal below.

Assumptions: goals under 5 years use a conservative profile (4% expected return, 5% volatility); 5–15 years use moderate (6%, 10%); 15+ years use aggressive (8%, 15%). Inflation is assumed at 3%/year. Each goal is run through 800 randomized simulations to estimate the probability of reaching its inflation-adjusted target. This tool is for education and planning conversations only — it is not financial or legal advice.
